You might wonder why I've chosen 2 colours to explain this moment.

1:To let the moment speak - Just like the decisive moment captures a single second, the limited palette allows the photographs and their story to stand quietly, without distraction.

2:To create stillness - Black and white brings a calm, silent feeling, giving each image space to breathe and letting the viewer notice what truly matters.

3:To highlight contrast and meaning - The simplicity of two colors mirrors the tension and emotion in a fleeting moment, showing that even small differences can carry deep significance.
